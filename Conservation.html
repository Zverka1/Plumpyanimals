Why Polar Bears

Polar Bears are majestic creatures that live in the artic. Its latin name Ursus maritimus means 'sea bear' this is due to it spending most of its live around water either in it or on top of it on the sea ice. They fall under the status vulnerable and there are only around 20,000 of them left in the wild and there are many threats to the polar bear meaning this number is ever decreasing.
